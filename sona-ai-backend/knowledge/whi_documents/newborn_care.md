# WHO Newborn Health & Care Recommendations

## Official WHO Sources

- **WHO Recommendations on Newborn Health**
  - **Direct PDF Link:** [Download Guidelines (PDF)](https://iris.who.int/bitstream/handle/10665/259269/WHO-MCA-17.07-eng.pdf)
  - **Summary:** The definitive guide for newborn care immediately after birth. It includes strict clinical protocols on late cord clamping (1 to 3 minutes after birth), initiating skin-to-skin contact during the first hour to prevent hypothermia, Vitamin K prophylaxis, and neonatal resuscitation protocols.

- **WHO Newborn Health Overview**
  - **Resource Link:** [WHO Newborn Health Portal](https://www.who.int/health-topics/newborn-health)
  - **Summary:** Global targets and general guidance on protecting newborn health, preventing infections, and the "Every Newborn Action Plan" (ENAP).
