# WHO Maternal & Postnatal Care Guidelines

## Official WHO Sources

- **WHO Recommended Interventions for Improving Maternal and Newborn Health**
  - **Direct PDF Link:** [Download Guidelines (PDF)](https://iris.who.int/bitstreams/5cbd4eec-d3a7-4373-85ee-8a3448dcc850/download)
  - **Summary:** Covers routine essential care for all women and babies, including monitoring of pregnancy progress, tetanus immunization, iron/folic acid supplementation, and counseling on nutrition and breastfeeding.

- **WHO Recommendations on Maternal and Newborn Care for a Positive Postnatal Experience**
  - **Direct PDF Link:** [Download Guidelines (PDF)](https://iris.who.int/server/api/core/bitstreams/a6696f52-fa0f-42fc-bedd-ebfc8725b65b/content)
  - **Summary:** Details the WHO postnatal care model, which requires a minimum of four postnatal care contacts. It provides guidelines on maternal mental health, physical recovery (like relieving uterine cramping and pelvic floor muscle training), and transition to home care.
